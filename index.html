Seasonal Report

This website serves as an interactive portal in regards to our seasonal progress as a Zoöp. Our goals, our interventions and our commitment to the awareness of other beings.

In order to respect the needs of the surrounding ecosystem, Mediamatic needs to determine its personal regenerative goals. For this we use the Zoönomic Annual Cycle (ZAC), a tool developed by the Zoönomic Institute. It helps organisations review their impact and set priorities each year. Together with Jolanda Verspagen, we go through this cycle. These five goals can be found within the ecosytem of this website, along with our wildlife camera's, logbook, and annual Zoop plan.
